Privacy Policy — Wegweiser Extension

Last updated: 2026-02-06

Overview

Wegweiser is a browser extension that lets you interact with third‑party AI providers from a side panel and a dedicated Projects view. This policy explains what data is handled and how it’s used.

Data We Handle

  • User inputs: prompts, messages, and any text you paste or type.
  • Page content (optional): if you use “Summarize Page,” the extension may read the current page content with your permission.
  • Provider credentials: API keys and provisioning keys you enter.

How Data Is Used

  • To generate AI responses based on your prompts.
  • To provide summaries or other features you explicitly trigger.
  • To store local settings and preferences (e.g., model choice, favorites).

Data Storage

  • Local storage only: API keys and settings are stored locally in your browser.
  • Encrypted at rest: Sensitive local data is encrypted with a device-local key (no passphrase).
  • No sync to our servers: We do not run a backend for data collection.
  • Optional image storage: generated images may be stored locally in your browser’s IndexedDB cache, with an adjustable size limit.

Data Sharing

  • Third‑party AI providers: When you send a request, your input (and any optional page content you choose to process) is sent to the selected provider (OpenRouter).
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other parties.

Permissions

The extension requests permissions only to enable features you use (e.g., accessing page content for summarization, opening tabs for image viewing). You can disable features or uninstall the extension at any time.

Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you clear it via Options or uninstall the extension.
  • Image cache data is automatically pruned based on your configured storage limit.

Security

We aim to minimize data collection and keep data local whenever possible. No telemetry or analytics are collected.

Your Choices

  • You control which providers are enabled.
  • You can clear local data at any time in Options.
  • You can remove the extension to delete all local data.
